Inferences based on the analytics and research:

  1. We can notice that agricultural land distribution is not even acorss the country
  2. The cluster which has the largest agricultural land like Madhyapradesh, Maharastra are the poor performers in overall yeild
  3. The states that has comparatively less agricultural land area like Tamilnadu,Westbengal etc... have shown better performance in yeild

Reason for this unbalance Area vs yeild pattern:

seasson based sowing pattern of India

  1. We can notice that climate based agricultural sowing pattern is not even acorss the country
  2. The cluster which has the largest agricultural land like Madhyapradesh, Maharastra sow the crops only during Kharif seasson
  3. The states that has comparatively less agricultural land area like Tamilnadu,Westbengal etc... sow the crops during entire year
  4. This uneven sowing pattern and limited climate sowing is the main reason for the uneven yeild stat across the nation
